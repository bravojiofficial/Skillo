About Skillo

Recruitment has always been one of the pain points of any organisation. In present India, people across generations have suffered the lack of exposure especially in the less urbanised areas of the country. But slowly and systematically, the trends are changing. Recent developments imply that the recruitment industry is now spreading its wings in the tier-2 and tier-3 cities and hiring is getting more and more specifically. Various recruitment firms old and new are looking at expansion to these cities but many are not organized. A further magnified view reveals better facts. After enormous job creations in micro urban areas, maximum of recruitment firms are either not present or those are present they are not organized and we are specifically focused on that particular area with full of confidence and expertise with a organized manner. We are an innovative recruitment/HR services provider that connects human resources as per our client and market need, facilitates clients transcend business goals and aims at a sustainable symbiotic relationship that's helps organizations address issues like hiring, productivity and scale challenges.
